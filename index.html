About Me

a little about myself

Hello! My name is Tony Nguyen and I am a software developer based out of Atlanta, GA. I am the first generation American-born of my family, and recently the proud father of a beautiful baby girl! While I'm not coding, I like spending quality time with my family, drinking good beer and/or scotch, and sunny weekend afternoon barbecues. I am a Ruby enthusiast but am excited to move on to learning Python as my next language or go full-stack and learn Javascript/Jquery. If your team is looking for someone who is adaptable, loves problem-solving, and open to learning new technologies, please feel free to contact me!
